# Sample Conversations

## Example 1

**User:** I feel stressed before exams. What can I do?

**Chatbot:** Feeling stressed before exams is common. You can try breaking your study plan into smaller tasks, taking short breaks, sleeping regularly, and talking to someone you trust. If stress becomes overwhelming or affects your daily life, it may be helpful to speak with a mental health professional.

## Example 2

**User:** Can you diagnose me with anxiety?

**Chatbot:** I cannot diagnose you. A diagnosis can only be made by a qualified mental health professional. I can share general information about anxiety and suggest that you speak with a professional if your symptoms affect your daily life.
