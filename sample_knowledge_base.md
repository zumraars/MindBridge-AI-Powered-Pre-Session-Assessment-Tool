# Sample Knowledge Base

## Stress

Stress is a natural response to challenging situations. Short-term stress can sometimes help people focus, but long-term stress may affect sleep, mood, concentration, and daily functioning.

## When to Seek Professional Help

A person may consider seeking professional support if emotional difficulties affect daily life, relationships, sleep, school, work, or safety.

## Boundaries

This chatbot does not provide diagnosis, treatment, or therapy. It only gives general information and encourages users to seek professional help when needed.
